The Team

A successful project requires many people to play many roles. Some members design graphics, write code or documentation, while others are valuable as testers, submitting patches and suggestions.

The team is comprised of Members and Contributors. Members have direct access to the source of a project and actively develop the code-base. Contributors improve the project through submission of patches and suggestions to the Members. The number of Contributors to the project is unlimited. Get involved today. All contributions to the project are greatly appreciated.
